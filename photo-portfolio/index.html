About

I Photograph
What Feels
Real

Based in New York, I'm a photographer drawn to the spaces between moments — the breath before a laugh, the glance that says everything. My work blends documentary instinct with a fine-art eye.

Every shoot is a collaboration. I want you to feel at ease, not performed.
