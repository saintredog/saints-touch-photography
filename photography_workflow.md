
# Photography Client Follow-Up Workflow

**Goals:**
- Increase booking rate
- Respond faster to inquiries
- Follow up with people who stop replying
- Get testimonials after shoots

**Tools in use:** Instagram DMs, phone text, email

## Workflow Stages & Follow-Ups

### 1. New Inquiry (Instagram DM)
*   **Initial Response (Template):**
    "Hey [Client Name]! Thanks for reaching out! I'd love to help. To get started, please browse my portfolio here: https://www.saintstouch.photography/portfolio. Once you've had a look, tell me what kind of shoot you're envisioning, your desired timeframe, and your general budget or if you're looking for packages starting around [Your Starting Price]. This helps me suggest the perfect package for you! You can then proceed to book directly through my website."

### 2. After Portfolio Review & Initial Qualification

*   **Follow-up 1 (24 hours - No Reply - via Instagram DM or Email):**
    "Hi [Client Name], checking in regarding your photography inquiry. After reviewing my portfolio and packages, have you had a chance to consider which option works best? I'm here to answer any questions you might have before you book."

*   **Follow-up 2 (48 hours - No Reply - via Instagram DM or Email):**
    "Hi [Client Name], following up on your photography inquiry. If you're still considering your options, I'm happy to clarify any details about my portfolio or booking process. If your plans have shifted, just let me know."

*   **Follow-up 3 (7-10 days - No Reply - Reactivation - via Email):**
    "Hi [Client Name], thought I'd reach out one more time. Sometimes schedules get busy, and I understand if photography plans are on hold. If you're still looking to book a session after seeing my portfolio, I'd love to connect. If not, no pressure – I wish you the best with your search!"

### 3. After Shoot to Request Review (via Email or Text)

*   **Follow-up (24-48 hours after photo delivery):**
    "Hi [Client Name], I hope you're loving your photos! It was a pleasure working with you. I'd be so grateful if you could take a moment to leave a review of your experience with [Your Photography Business Name] here: [Link to Google My Business/Website Testimonial Page/Specific Review Platform]. Your feedback helps me grow!"

---

## Short Notes on How to Use It:

*   **Prompt Response:** Respond to new DMs quickly using the "New Inquiry" template.
*   **Track Leads:** Use the "Simple Lead Tracker" to log client details and follow-up dates.
*   **Consistent Follow-ups:** Stick to the 24-hour, 48-hour, and 7-10 day schedule to re-engage potential clients.
*   **Encourage Reviews:** Always send the post-delivery review request to build social proof.
*   **Personalize:** While templates are provided, always personalize where possible.
