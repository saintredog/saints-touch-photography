Service — 02

P&L Diagnostic & Turnaround

A forensic 14-day audit of your revenue engine. We find the margin leaks you've stopped seeing — and build the fix plan.

The Problem

The business looks fine on the surface. It isn't.

Most P&L problems aren't accounting failures — they're strategy failures that show up in the numbers. Pricing erosion. Customer concentration. Sales inefficiency. Cost creep normalized over years. These don't fix themselves.

"Every business I've walked into had at least two or three structural issues the leadership team had learned to live with. They're not unsolvable. They're just invisible from the inside."

— Talha Syed

The 14-Day Audit is deliberately time-compressed. Speed forces clarity. By Day 14, you will have a complete picture of your commercial performance gaps — and a ranked list of the highest-ROI interventions.

The 14-Day Audit

Structured engagement. Hard findings. Actionable output.

  • Revenue segmentation by customer, product, and channel
  • Gross margin analysis by SKU and segment
  • Sales cycle efficiency and deal-velocity mapping
  • Pricing model and discount policy review
  • Customer retention and expansion economics
  • Overhead structure vs. revenue trajectory
  • Top-5 intervention priorities with ROI estimates
Request the Audit
How It Works
Day 1–3

Data Ingestion & Stakeholder Interviews

We review your financial statements, CRM data, and pricing models. We interview your CFO, CRO, and top-three account managers.

Day 4–9

Diagnostic Analysis

Pattern identification across revenue, margin, customer, and cost dimensions. We build the complete picture of your commercial engine.

Day 10–12

Intervention Design

We design the fix — not just identify the problem. Each intervention includes ownership, timeline, and ROI projection.

Day 13–14

Executive Readout

Full findings presented to your board or leadership team. Findings document and 90-day execution roadmap delivered.

About Magnus

Leadership & Advisory Philosophy

Magnus is built on one principle: senior expertise, directly applied. No delegation to junior teams. No slide-deck strategy without operational follow-through.

TS
Talha Syed
Founder & Managing Principal, Magnus Strategic Advisory
P&L Leadership International Expansion Miller Heiman Board Advisory FinTech

Talha Syed brings over 25 years of operational leadership across the global financial technology, currency management, and enterprise software industries. His career has been defined by taking complex commercial challenges — revenue stagnation, market entry risk, organizational misalignment — and building the structures that resolve them permanently.

At NCR, Diebold-Nixdorf, Glory, Hitachi, and De La Rue, Talha led large-scale P&L functions, built international sales organizations, and drove cross-border expansion into markets across Europe, the Middle East, India, and Asia-Pacific. He has worked directly with C-suites, boards, and government institutions across four continents.

Magnus was founded on the conviction that the most valuable strategic advisory happens at the intersection of operational depth and executive perspective — and that too many firms settle for consultants who have never actually run the business they're advising on.

"Strategy without accountability is just analysis. I engage where the work meets the revenue — and I stay until it's done."

— Talha Syed
