Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Driven by Passion

Ember Coffee Co. was born out of a simple belief — that great coffee changes the way you experience your morning. Founded in 2019 by two lifelong coffee obsessives, we set out to build a space where craft meets community.

We travel to origin farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, building relationships with the farmers who grow our beans. Everything is roasted in-house, in small batches, with care.

Direct-trade relationships with 12 farms
In-house small-batch roasting, daily
Barista-trained staff, SCA certified
Zero waste commitment since 2021
