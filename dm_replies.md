# Instagram DM Replies for Photography Sales

--- 

## 1. New Inquiry (Initial Response)

"Hey [Client Name]! Thanks for reaching out! I'd love to help. To get started, please browse my portfolio here: https://www.saintstouch.photography/portfolio. Once you've had a look, could you tell me more about: 
*   What kind of shoot are you envisioning? 
*   When are you hoping to have it? 
*   Where would the shoot take place? 
*   Do you have a general budget, or are you comfortable with packages starting around [Your Starting Price]? 
*   What's the main purpose of this shoot? 
*   Any special requests? 
*   And how did you hear about me?

This helps me suggest the perfect package for you! You can then proceed to book directly through my website."

## 2. 24-Hour Follow-up

"Hi [Client Name], checking in regarding your photography inquiry. After reviewing my portfolio and packages, have you had a chance to consider which option works best? I'm here to answer any questions you might have before you book."

## 3. 48-Hour Follow-up

"Hi [Client Name], following up one last time on your photography inquiry. If you're still exploring options, I'm happy to clarify any details about my portfolio or booking process. If your plans have shifted, just let me know."

## 4. Reactivation (7-10 Days No Reply)

"Hi [Client Name], thought I'd reach out one more time. Sometimes schedules get busy, and I understand if photography plans are on hold. If you're still looking to book a session after seeing my portfolio, I'd love to connect. If not, no pressure – I wish you the best with your search!"

## 5. Booking Confirmation

"Fantastic, [Client Name]! Your booking for [Shoot Type] on [Date] at [Time] is confirmed! I'm so excited to work with you. I'll send over a quick prep guide shortly. Let me know if you have any questions in the meantime!"

## 6. Review Request (Post-Delivery)

"Hi [Client Name], I hope you're loving your photos! It was a pleasure working with you. I'd be so grateful if you could take a moment to leave a review of your experience with [Your Photography Business Name] here: [Link to Google My Business/Website Testimonial Page/Specific Review Platform]. Your feedback helps me grow!"
