The Photographer

Clifford Roberts III

I'm Clifford, the eye behind Saint's Touch Photography. What started as experimenting with Photoshop edits in 2020 grew into a passion for visual storytelling. Based in Virginia Beach, VA — I specialize in portraits, boudoir, maternity, fashion, and events. My style blends cinematic depth with authentic emotion. Every session is intentional. Every image is crafted. Available across Hampton Roads and for destination shoots worldwide.
