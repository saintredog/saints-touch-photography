Our Story

Who We Are

The name Payteck comes from a simple idea — "Pay the tech for their services." That vision took root in 2022 and officially launched in the summer of 2024.

We specialize in computer repair, sales, data wiping, recycling, and education for everyone. Our mission is to service your everyday computing needs — no matter your background or experience level.

We have a strong passion for getting devices into homes and empowering individuals who may feel left behind by technology.

"Our main goal is to service your every day computer needs — and to empower everyone with the technology skills they deserve."
