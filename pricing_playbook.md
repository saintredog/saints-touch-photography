# Photography Pricing Playbook

To ensure we're a good fit and to maximize booking potential, follow these steps to qualify a lead before sending detailed pricing:

1.  **Initial Inquiry & Portfolio Review:**
    *   Direct new inquiries to your portfolio (https://www.saintstouch.photography/portfolio) first.
    *   Collect initial information: shoot type, desired timeframe, location, and a general budget or comfort with starting prices.

2.  **Qualify Basic Needs:**
    *   Review the collected information against your services and availability.
    *   Ensure the requested shoot type aligns with your expertise.
    *   Confirm the timeframe is feasible.
    *   Assess if their budget aligns with your general pricing structure (comfort with starting prices is a good indicator).

3.  **Address Key Questions (If Needed):**
    *   If there are major gaps or concerns (e.g., highly unusual shoot type, unrealistic timeframe), address them briefly to see if expectations can be aligned.

4.  **Personalized Package Suggestion:**
    *   Based on the qualified information, select 1-2 relevant packages from your offerings.
    *   Highlight key features and benefits that directly address their stated needs and purpose of the shoot.
    *   Avoid sending your entire pricing guide without qualification; this can overwhelm clients.

5.  **Set Clear Next Steps:**
    *   Invite them to ask questions about the suggested packages.
    *   Guide them to your booking process or a consultation call if preferred.

**Goal:** Only send detailed pricing once you have a clear understanding of the client's needs and have confirmed a basic fit, saving time and increasing the likelihood of booking a truly compatible client.
