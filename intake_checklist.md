# Photography Lead Intake Checklist

To effectively qualify and serve a new lead, ensure you collect the following information:

1.  **Client Name:** Full name of the potential client.
2.  **Instagram Handle:** Their Instagram username (for DM communication and tracking).
3.  **Shoot Type:** The specific type of photography session they are interested in (e.g., maternity, portrait, couple, brand, event, product).
4.  **Desired Shoot Date or Timeframe:** When they are hoping to have the photoshoot.
5.  **Location of Shoot:** Preferred location or type of location for the shoot.
6.  **Budget or Starting Price Comfort:** Their general budget range, or confirmation that they are comfortable with your starting package prices.
7.  **Purpose of the Shoot:** What the photos will be used for (e.g., personal use, website, marketing).
8.  **Any Special Requests:** Specific ideas, poses, props, or themes they have in mind.
9.  **How They Found the Photographer:** (e.g., Instagram, referral, Google, specific hashtag).
