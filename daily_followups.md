# Daily Photography Lead Follow-up Checklist

--- 

## 1. New Leads (Today's Inquiries)

*   Review new Instagram DMs.
*   For each new inquiry, use the "Initial Response" DM template.
*   Record the lead in your `lead_tracker.md`.

## 2. 24-Hour Follow-ups

*   Check `lead_tracker.md` for leads that received an initial response yesterday but haven't replied.
*   Send "Follow-up 1 (24 hours)" DM/Email.
*   Update "Last Reply Date" and "Next Follow-up Date" in `lead_tracker.md`.

## 3. 48-Hour Follow-ups

*   Check `lead_tracker.md` for leads that received the 24-hour follow-up yesterday but haven't replied.
*   Send "Follow-up 2 (48 hours)" DM/Email.
*   Update "Last Reply Date" and "Next Follow-up Date" in `lead_tracker.md`.

## 4. 7-10 Day Reactivation Leads

*   Check `lead_tracker.md` for leads that have been unresponsive for 7-10 days.
*   Send "Follow-up 3 (7-10 days - Reactivation)" Email.
*   Update "Last Reply Date" and "Next Follow-up Date" in `lead_tracker.md`.

---

**Important:** Always refer to `photography_workflow.md` for the exact DM/Email templates.
